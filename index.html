HAKUNA MATATA

Hakuna Matata is a Swahili phrase that translates to "no worries" or "no problems" in English. It gained worldwide recognition and popularity through Disney's animated film "The Lion King," where it was featured prominently in the song "Hakuna Matata." In the context of the film and the song, it represents a carefree and joyful attitude, encouraging characters to embrace a worry-free lifestyle.
